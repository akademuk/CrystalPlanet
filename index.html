Who we are

WealthGuard is a Dubai-based investment and management ecosystem, operating across food & beverage, finance, and commercial real estate, with a core focus on income-generating assets and value creation through active management.

The launch of the W.Investment Property Fund represents the next phase of the platform, targeting commercial real estate opportunities with strong cash flow potential, followed by stabilization and exit of institutional-quality assets, developed and managed in-house.

Founder.

Konstantin Harbuz is the Founder and CEO of WealthGuard, responsible for the strategic direction of the platform, investment decisions and execution of the fund's development strategy.

Commitment

Founder Commitment

The Founder is fully committed to the fund's success, providing strategic leadership, active management and full responsibility for investment execution and results. The Founder maintains direct oversight of the platform's strategy, investment decisions and project delivery, as well as personal due diligence of each LP.

Co-Investment

WealthGuard and its affiliated companies co-invest alongside investors in each project, ensuring strong alignment of interests and long-term commitment to the success of the investments.

In-House Construction

The development process is managed by our in-house platform, which is responsible for project planning, contractor management and construction delivery, ensuring quality control, cost discipline and timeline execution, avoiding common delays and unprofessionalism.

In house master real estate

Tenant sourcing, leasing strategy and tenant mix creation are managed by our internal team Flipping Fund Realty with practical experience in retail operations and commercial leasing.

Professional troubleshooting

Each investment undergoes a structured due diligence process covering legal, financial, technical and commercial analysis before approval.

Partners & Vendors

Holding collaborates with established professional partners and service providers across legal, financial, construction and asset management disciplines.
